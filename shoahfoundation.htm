The USC Shoah Foundation

The whole point of the Shoah Foundation is to teach about past genocides and to remember the stories of those who lived through them. Each year a select few are allowed to embark on this

learning expierence. I was given the opportunity to do this twice (once before Covid and once during). Each time I did it I learned more about the past, and I was able to have a deeper

understanding of the genocides that were talked about. One of my favorite things about this expierence was being able to go to a mueseum that outlined the Japanese-American expierence

during World War II. The USC Shoah Foundation was eye opening and quite enjoyable.
